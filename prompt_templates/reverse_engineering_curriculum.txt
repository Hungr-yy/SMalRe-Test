REVERSE ENGINEERING CURRICULUM TEMPLATES
=========================================
Multi-step reasoning task templates for teacher-generated training examples
in the SMalA knowledge distillation pipeline.

These templates guide the teacher LLM when generating curriculum examples
targeting specific weaknesses identified during student evaluation.

==========================================================================

==========================================================================
NOTES ON TEMPLATE USAGE
==========================================================================

1. Templates are rendered by teacher_engine.TeacherEngine.generate_curriculum()
   with weakness-specific context injected into {placeholder} fields.

2. The teacher should vary difficulty across the curriculum:
   - 30% Easy examples for foundational knowledge
   - 50% Medium examples for core reasoning skills
   - 20% Hard examples for advanced pattern recognition

3. When generating Hard examples, prefer:
   - Multi-stage attacks with overlapping techniques
   - Obfuscated or packed binaries
   - Living-off-the-land (LOtL) techniques
   - Novel evasion techniques not seen in training

4. Example counts per distillation round:
   - Quick iteration / debugging : 100–500
   - Standard training round     : 1,000–10,000
   - Full production curriculum  : 10,000–100,000

5. All generated examples should be reviewed for:
   - Technical accuracy (MITRE IDs, API names, file paths)
   - Realistic but non-sensitive content
   - Diversity of malware families and techniques
